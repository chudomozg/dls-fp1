How does this App Work?

So what's under the hood? There is Artificial intelligence based on a neural network. There are more details below

There is Mask RCNN model with ResNet-50 backbone pretrained on MS COCO trai 2017 dataset + FPN
I used Detectorn2. It's is Facebook AI Research's software. In detail, detectron2 was based on Pytorch and torchvision
What dataset was the model trained? it's the Cityspaces dataset, you can read more detail's about it here https://www.cityscapes-dataset.com/
